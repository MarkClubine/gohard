About GOHARD World

GOHARD World was founded in 2026 on a single, unshakeable principle: that half-measures are for people who only go medium.

What began as a rhinestone tee and a dream has grown into a full luxury streetwear house spanning apparel, footwear, accessories, motorsport equipment, and a fleet of vehicles that exist purely to make a point.

Every GOHARD piece is designed for the individual who looks at the line between too much and not enough and thinks: "I should cross that line, and then keep going."

Uncompromising

We do not make "casual" pieces. Every garment is an event. Every accessory is a statement. If it doesn't turn heads, it doesn't ship.

Excessive

Where others see "too many rhinestones," we see "not enough rhinestones." Where others see "too many spikes," we see an opportunity.

Relentless

GOHARD is not a clothing brand. It is a lifestyle, a philosophy, and—if you squint hard enough—a religion. We do not stop. We do not rest. We go hard.

Motorsport Division

Because going hard on foot was never going to be enough.
