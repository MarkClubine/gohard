Spring 2026: New Season, Same Intensity

The GOHARD Spring 2026 collection draws inspiration from the streets where legends are made. Every thread, every stitch, every rhinestone is placed with the precision of someone who refuses to phone it in.

This season introduces softer palettes—blush pinks, cloud whites—paired with the aggressive hardware that defines the GOHARD DNA. The result is a contradiction that somehow makes perfect sense: tender brutality, gentle aggression, soft power.

The Leather Bib: A Manifesto

Fine dining is an act of commitment. So is wearing a full-grain Italian leather bib embossed with a lifestyle brand logo to a three-star restaurant. The GOHARD Leather Bib is not an accessory. It is a declaration.

Handcrafted from the same leather used in luxury automotive interiors, the bib features the signature GOHARD emblem in forest green. It says: I am here to eat, and I am here to go hard while doing it.

The Flagship: A Temple of Hardness

Located at the intersection of ambition and audacity, the GOHARD World flagship store is a monument to the lifestyle. Clad in blackened concrete and glass, the space was designed by architects who were simply told: "Make it go hard."

Inside, visitors encounter the full GOHARD universe—from rhinestone-encrusted basics to spiked outerwear, all presented in a retail environment that feels less like shopping and more like a pilgrimage.
